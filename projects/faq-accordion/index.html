Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find my purpose in life?
You discover your purpose in life by discovering yourself. Understand that you don't live a life, you create a life. In life, you are not a detective, you are an artist. You have to create something beautiful, exciting, God-centred, fruitful. Now, get to work. Work on yourself! By working on yourself, you discover more about yourself and then you are directed.
What do I even have to start with?
Beyond all that you have been blessed with, you have been given greatest life resources, they are some of your best. They are time, energy and opportunity. How are you using those? Ask yourself. Given your energy, health, skills, vibrancy, how are you converting your endless opportunities within the time frames or seasons you have been given?
Hmmm, this is deep. How do I also get as wise as this?
If you are looking for anything in life. Seek God. He is the owner and giver of everything. Seek the wisdom of God. Pray for it. Study for it. Search for it. Meditate on it. Work for it. Become it.
Who is God?
He is the giver of your spirit.
